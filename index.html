Kessoku Band (結束バンド) is the main unit band of Bocchi the Rock! series.
The name "Kessoku Band" is a pun made by Ryō Yamada on "Kessoku" (unity) and "Kessoku Band" (cable tie).
The band has been drawn as the center of this work.
The unit band is based in live house STARRY,
which is based on the real-life club named "Shelter" in Shimokitazawa, Setagaya ward, Tokyo.
